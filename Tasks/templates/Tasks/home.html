Working as One

How the Collaboration Works

  • Institution publishes diploma competency requirements
  • Organizations map training duties to those requirements
  • Supervisors assign tasks aligned to the calendar plan
  • Students complete structured duties for diploma credit
  • Automated reports verify compliance for graduation and organization submit the report direct to the institution
